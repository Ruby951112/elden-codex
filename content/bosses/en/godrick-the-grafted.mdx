## Phase Strategy

Godrick is your first Shardbearer and your first true taste of FromSoftware's spectacle. The fight is structured as a teaching moment — the developers want you to learn lock-on management, summon utility, and patience against telegraphed combos. Take it slow.

### Phase I — The Grafted King

Godrick wields an oversized battle-axe and a cargo of stitched-on limbs. His axe attacks are huge but slow. The threat in this phase is not damage — it is positioning. Godrick frequently rolls toward you, repositions, and forces you to break lock-on or get caught mid-step.

> **Critical:** Summon **Nepheli Loux** at the golden sign just inside the fog gate. She is one of the strongest NPC summons in the game. She will tank phase 1 almost single-handedly while you punish from behind.

The axe attacks worth memorizing:

- **Two-handed downward slam** — Roll to either side just before impact. Punish with 2 R1s.
- **Sweeping ground slam** — Telegraphed by him planting the axe. Roll forward through the shockwave.
- **Whirlwind spin** — He rotates 3-4 times. Stay close; the inner ring of the spin is safe.

### Phase II — Dragon's Strength

At ~40% HP, Godrick grafts a dragon's head onto his right arm. He drops to one knee for the cinematic. **Heal during this animation.** It is one of the longest free windows in the game.

The dragon arm changes everything:

- **Fire cone breath** — Sprint perpendicular to him. Do not roll; rolling is too slow to clear the cone.
- **Fire stomp** — He plants the dragon head into the ground. Roll backward, not toward.
- **Combined axe + fire spin** — Rare but lethal. Keep your distance and bait his axe-only spin instead.

Phase 2 punishes greed harder than phase 1. Limit yourself to single R1s. The dragon arm has its own hitbox and will catch you on follow-throughs you'd survive in phase 1.

## Attacks & Counters

| Attack | Counter |
|--------|---------|
| Axe overhead slam | Roll left or right, never back. Punish 2 R1s. |
| Axe whirlwind (4 spins) | Stay inside his hitbox. Roll on the final spin. |
| Body roll | He literally rolls across the arena. Sidestep, do not chase. |
| Limb throw | Throws a stitched arm. Strafe-walk left, do not roll. |
| Fire breath cone (P2) | Sprint perpendicular, then roll into his side as the cone ends. |
| Fire stomp (P2) | Roll backward. The shockwave radius is larger than the visual. |

## Recommended Loadout

- **Spirit Ash:** None needed if Nepheli is summoned. Otherwise: Lone Wolves +3 or any tanky melee summon.
- **Weapon:** Bleed weapons excel. Uchigatana with Seppuku Ash of War procs blood loss reliably.
- **Talismans:** Dragoncrest Greatshield Talisman (physical damage reduction), Crimson Amber Medallion.
- **Buffs:** Fire-resistant boluses for phase 2. Sacred Tear in flask helps with grafted scorpions.

## Lore Pause

Godrick is a tragic figure — last in Godwyn's bloodline, desperately grafting more limbs to seem stronger than he is. His final words ("Forgive me, Godfrey... ancestor!") hint at the dynasty's collapse. Don't kill the side-quest opportunity: speak to **Gostoc** at the gate and the entrance before defeating Godrick, and don't kill him. He has rewards later in Leyndell.

> *Godrick teaches patience. The fights ahead will demand it.*

## Related Bosses

Defeating Godrick grants the Great Rune of Godrick (+5 to all stats when activated at a Divine Tower). Your next destination is Liurnia — head north through the back of the throne room.
