## Phase Strategy

The Godskin Noble is a deceptive fight. Most players die in the first thirty seconds because they expect a slow, methodical duel — then the Noble inflates into a living wrecking ball and flattens them across the arena. Once you understand the roll mechanics, this becomes one of the most exploitable mid-game bosses in the game.

The Temple of Eiglay arena is rectangular with pillars scattered throughout. The pillars are decorative — they will not save you from the roll. Do not hide behind them. The fight demands positioning discipline, not terrain abuse.

> **Critical:** The Godskin Noble's roll attack has a blind spot directly at his left or right side. If you stand perpendicular to his roll direction and do not panic-dodge, he will miss you entirely. This is the most important mechanic in the fight.

### Understanding the Bloat

Below ~70% HP, the Noble can trigger his bloat phase at any time. He pauses, inflates like a balloon, and begins rolling. The roll lasts 10-15 seconds and deals catastrophic damage on contact — 60-80% of your max HP per hit. He can chain the roll up to three times in sequence if you let him.

The counter:

1. **Do not run away.** Running perpendicular keeps you in the roll's path. Sprint *toward* him diagonally until you are at his side.
2. **Stand still.** Once you are perpendicular to the roll direction, stop moving. The hitbox is a cylinder — if you are outside the forward projection, you are safe.
3. **Punish the deflation.** When the roll ends, he deflates in place for 2-3 seconds. This is a free charged R2 or jumping heavy attack.

If you master this counter, the fight becomes trivial. The Noble will spend half his HP bar rolling into walls while you stand still and heal.

## Attacks & Counters

| Attack | Counter |
|--------|---------|
| Rapier thrust combo (3-hit) | Roll into him on the 2nd thrust. Punish after the 3rd. He always ends with a step-back. |
| Spinning slash | He winds up for 1 second. Roll forward through him on the spin's apex. Punish 2 R1s. |
| Black Flame toss (ranged) | Strafe-walk left. Do not roll — the flame travels slower than your walk speed. |
| Belly slam | He raises both arms and drops forward. Roll backward. The hitbox extends slightly beyond his body. |
| Bloat roll | Sprint diagonally toward his side. Stand perpendicular to the roll. Do not dodge. Punish deflation with charged R2. |
| Flying belly flop | Rare. He floats upward and drops. Sprint away immediately when he leaves the ground. |

The rapier combo is his most common attack. The third hit has a long recovery — this is your primary punish window in non-bloat phases. Do not greed more than two R1s. The Noble's poise is moderate, but he will hyper-armor through your third swing and counter-thrust.

## Recommended Loadout

The Godskin Noble is weak to two things: **Black Flame incantations** and **posture breaks**.

- **Weapon:** Any heavy weapon with high stance damage. **Prelate's Inferno Crozier**, **Greatsword**, or **Giant-Crusher** work exceptionally well. If you lack strength, **Moonveil** or **Rivers of Blood** proc bleed reliably.
- **Spirit Ash:** **Mimic Tear +10** or **Black Knife Tiche +10**. The Noble's aggro switches frequently — a summon that can dodge independently (Tiche) or match your DPS (Mimic) shortens the fight significantly.
- **Talismans:**
  - **Dragoncrest Greatshield Talisman** (reduces physical damage from the roll if you mistime positioning)
  - **Bull-Goat's Talisman** (poise — survive clipping hits during punishes)
  - **Green Turtle Talisman** (stamina regen for sprint management during roll phase)
  - **Fire Scorpion Charm** (if using Black Flame incantations)
- **Incantations:** **Black Flame** or **Scouring Black Flame** deal bonus damage and apply percentage-based HP burn. The Noble is categorized as a god, making Black Flame incantations deal ~30% more damage than their base values suggest.
- **Items:** **Neutralizing Boluses** ×5. The Noble's Black Flame tosses inflict minor Death Blight buildup. Rare, but lethal if you eat multiple fireballs in sequence.

If you are running a pure melee build with no access to Black Flame, focus on **posture breaking**. Guard counters with heavy weapons stagger the Noble in 3-4 hits. A successful riposte deals ~20% of his HP bar.

## Tactics by Build Type

**Strength/Quality:** Stay aggressive. Jump attacks build stance damage faster than R1s. Bait the rapier combo, punish with jumping R2. Ignore the roll — stand at his side and heal during it.

**Dex/Bleed:** **Corpse Piler** (Rivers of Blood Ash of War) procs hemorrhage in ~4 full combos. The Noble has no bleed resistance. If you can survive long enough to proc twice, the fight is over.

**Sorcery:** **Comet Azur** trivializes the fight if you have enough FP flasks. Summon Mimic, drink **Cerulean Hidden Tear** flask, cast Comet during his rapier combo recovery. He will die before the beam ends.

**Faith:** **Black Flame** spam. Stand at mid-range, cast on cooldown. The percentage burn stacks. He will lose 10% of his HP bar per cast over the next 5 seconds. You can kill him in under 90 seconds without melee.

## Lore Pause

The Godskin Noble is one of the apostles who serve the Gloam-Eyed Queen — an outer god antithetical to the Erdtree. The "godskin" in his name is literal. The robes are stitched from the skin of gods he has flayed. His rapier was forged in the age before the Erdtree's dominion, when destined death was not yet sealed.

You will fight Godskin apostles repeatedly across the game. Learn this fight now. The duo encounter in Crumbling Farum Azula will test your mastery of both the Noble and the Apostle simultaneously.

> *The Noble's roll is not a threat. It is an invitation to punish him.* Resist the urge to panic, and the fight will come to you.

## Related Bosses

After defeating the Godskin Noble, continue deeper into Mt. Gelmir toward **Volcano Manor**. The path leads to **Rykard, Lord of Blasphemy** — a gimmick fight with a provided legendary weapon. The Noble's runes (36,000) are best spent on smithing stones at the Twin Maiden Husks in Roundtable Hold.
