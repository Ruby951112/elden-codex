## Overview

Limgrave is where every Tarnished wakes up, and FromSoftware designed it to teach you the language of the game through open-world discovery rather than hand-holding. The western half — the grassy, sun-soaked cliffs around the Church of Elleh and Gatefront Ruins — is genuinely forgiving. The eastern half, Mistwood, is darker, more wooded, and home to stronger enemies that will punish overconfidence. Stormveil Castle, which caps the region's main story beat, is a genuine difficulty spike compared to everything else in Limgrave, so treat the open world as mandatory homework before you knock on Margit's door. Threat level overall: low, with pockets of "you probably shouldn't be here yet."

## Recommended Approach

When you exit the Stranded Graveyard tutorial, resist the urge to charge the golden Tree Sentinel on horseback. Instead, turn left toward the **Church of Elleh** to meet **Merchant Kale**, grab the **Crafting Kit**, and buy the **Torch** if you're playing a non-magic build. Rest at the Site of Grace here to trigger **Renna's** appearance later, which unlocks Spirit Calling and the **Lone Wolf Ashes** — your single best early investment.

From Elleh, ride north to the **Gatefront Ruins** to find the **Whetstone Knife** in a chest underground, unlocking Ash of War socketing. This one item meaningfully expands your build options for the rest of the run.

Work through western Limgrave methodically. Clear **Groveside Cave** (Beastman of Farum Azula) for easy Runes and a **Flamedrake Talisman**. Explore **Murkwater Cave** to trigger the **Patches** event — don't kill him; he opens a shop. Hit the **Coastal Cave** to unlock access to the **Church of Dragon Communion** on the offshore island, which sells Dragon Communion incantations if you killed Agheel.

For Mistwood and the eastern section, listen for the wolf howling near the **Mistwood Ruins**, then gesture **Finger Snap** (learned from Kale) to bring down **Blaidd**. His questline eventually leads to some of the best mid-game content in the Lands Between. While in the east, handle the **Summonwater Village Tibia Mariner** — it's trivial and drops the **Deathroot**, which you can give to **Gurranq** at the Bestial Sanctum for powerful Bestial incantations.

Once you're around level 20–25, take the evergaols. **Bloodhound Knight Darriwil** at the Forlorn Hound Evergaol is a mandatory stop if you're helping Blaidd and rewards the **Bloodhound's Fang**, which remains competitive through mid-game for Dexterity builds.

## Key Items

- **Lone Wolf Ashes** — Given by Renna at the Church of Elleh after you've rested at the Gatefront Grace. Your most reliable summon through the entire early game.
- **Whetstone Knife** — Chest in the basement of Gatefront Ruins. Unlocks Ash of War application at any Site of Grace. Do not skip this.
- **Bloodhound's Fang** — Reward for defeating Bloodhound Knight Darriwil. A Curved Greatsword with built-in bleed and a superb weapon art; carries Dex builds deep into the mid-game.
- **Flamedrake Talisman** — Drops from Beastman of Farum Azula in Groveside Cave. Fire resistance talisman, particularly useful against Agheel and Godrick's fire attacks.
- **Erdsteel Dagger** — Found in the Gatefront Ruins camp on a corpse. Excellent for Faith builds as it scales with Faith as its primary stat.

## Boss Order

1. **Soldier of Godrick** — Tutorial boss. Exists to confirm you understand blocking and attacking. Kill it.
2. **Beastman of Farum Azula** (Groveside Cave) — First real minor dungeon. Introduces the cave loop format.
3. **Flying Dragon Agheel** — Tackle him on horseback once you've got Torrent. Stay under his belly, bait fire breath, and punish. Drops a Dragon Heart for Dragon Communion.
4. **Patches** — Not a real fight; let it play out for the merchant unlock.
5. **Bloodhound Knight Darriwil** — Evergaol fight with a fast, aggressive knight. Summon Blaidd here for an easier time and to progress his quest.
6. **Tibia Mariner** (Summonwater Village) — Slow, passive, basically free Runes and a Deathroot.
7. **Ancestor Spirit / Regal Ancestor Spirit** (Nokron/Hallowhorn Grounds) — Optional, but worth doing if you've lit the eight bonfires in **Mistwood's Hallowhorn Grounds**. Beautiful fight, solid Remembrance reward.
8. **Tree Sentinel** — Skip him initially. Come back at level 20+ once you've got a +3 or +4 weapon and a clear idea of your build. Drops the **Golden Halberd**, one of the best early Faith weapons.
9. **Margit, the Fell Omen** — Gateway to Stormveil. Use **Margit's Shackle** (buy from Patches) to briefly stun him in Phase 1. Summon Jellyfish Spirit or Lone Wolf, and pressure his second phase hard.
10. **Godrick the Grafted** — Equip the Flamedrake Talisman, dodge into his swings, and when he grafts a dragon arm in Phase 2, stay on his left side. Drops the **Godrick's Great Rune** and **Remembrance of the Grafted**.

## Exit Path

After claiming Godrick's Great Rune and activating it at the **Limgrave Tower** Divine Tower east of Stormveil, you have two natural next steps. The **Grand Lift of Dectus** route takes you through **Liurnia of the Lakes** — reached by exiting north through Stormveil's back gate. Liurnia is broader, weirder, and home to Raya Lucaria Academy. Alternatively, explore **Weeping Peninsula** to the south of Limgrave for a self-contained detour with the **Castle Morne** dungeon and the **Leonine Misbegotten** boss, which is good Rune farming and yields the **Grafted Blade Greatsword**. Most players head to Liurnia first.
